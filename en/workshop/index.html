The Situation

Does This Sound Familiar?

You have an idea that won't leave your head. Maybe even five. But instead of acting, you drift — reading articles, asking friends, making plans that never launch.

Or you've already started. You've invested time, maybe money. But clients aren't coming and you don't know why.

In the age of AI, anyone can generate 10 business ideas before lunch. The tools to create have never been more accessible. But execution? It hasn't gotten easier or cheaper.

Most ideas don't fail because they're bad — they fail because nobody tested them before investing months into something the market never asked for.

Program

What We'll Do in 2 Hours

This workshop is not a lecture. You'll work. In two hours you'll get a methodology for rapid validation and a concrete plan to reach your first paying customers — before investing months and thousands of euros into something the market never asked for.

1

Test the Problem

Understand whether the pain you're solving is strong enough for people to pay for it.

2

Find the Customer

Build a profile of your first customer. Not an abstract "target group" but a specific person with a specific need and budget.

3

Gather Feedback

Learn a framework for rapid market validation — without building a product.

4

Map the Path

Create a plan for the shortest route to your first sale.

You work in a group of 25 entrepreneurs. The feedback you get here in 2 hours can save you months of guessing.
